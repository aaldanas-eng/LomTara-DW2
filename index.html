Our Story & Heritage

Lom Tara was founded with a deep respect for the ancient healing traditions of Thailand. Our journey began when our founder, Siriporn Nakamura, trained under master healers in the temples of Northern Thailand, learning the sacred art of Nuad Thai - traditional Thai massage.

With over 15 years of experience, we bring authentic Thai healing techniques directly to your home, creating a sanctuary of peace and wellness wherever you are. Our mission is to share the profound benefits of traditional Thai massage with busy individuals who deserve moments of tranquility in their daily lives.

Every session is more than just a massage - it's a journey back to balance, combining ancient wisdom with modern convenience to restore your body, mind, and spirit.
